Travel Tips & Must-Visit Places

  • Pack light and smart. Roll your clothes to save space.
  • Always carry a power bank and a universal adapter.
  • Learn a few local phrases—it goes a long way with the locals!
  • **Must-Visit:** The Taj Mahal in Agra, India; the Eiffel Tower in Paris, France; the Great Barrier Reef in Australia.
  • Stay hydrated, especially when traveling to hot climates.
  • Try the local cuisine. It's one of the best ways to experience a new culture.
