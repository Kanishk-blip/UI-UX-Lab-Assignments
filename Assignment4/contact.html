Frequently Asked Questions

Who can participate in TechFest 2025?

Students from any college can participate in our events. We welcome all disciplines and skill levels.

Is there a registration fee?

No, there is no registration fee for participants. It's completely free to register and attend!

What are the prizes for the competitions?

We offer exciting cash prizes, certificates, and trophies for the winners and runners-up of each competition.
